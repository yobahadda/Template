The Waves of Expression

Podcast Theme Guests

Podcast Theme

The podcast revolves around the right to access information for individuals with disabilities who speak the Amazigh language. It explores how this right impacts their freedom of expression and active participation in society, including expressing their opinions and engaging in public dialogue. Additionally, the podcast addresses the challenges they face in accessing information and proposes solutions to overcome these challenges.

Topic Description

The impact of the right to access information on the freedom of expression for individuals with disabilities.

Perspective of Discussion

  • Researcher (Student)
  • Public Employee
  • Civil Society Activist

Note: All guests are individuals with special needs.
