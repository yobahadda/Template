Welcome to the Digital Clinic!

In the dynamic digital landscape of Morocco, the Digital Clinic stands as a beacon of empowerment and skill enhancement. Our project is dedicated to providing comprehensive digital skills training tailored to the specific needs of diverse stakeholders across the country. Through rigorous research and collaboration with local partners, we've identified key areas where digital proficiency can drive significant positive impact, aligning with Morocco's national priorities and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

  • Journalism Trainees: Ensuring digital safety and literacy among future journalists is critical. Our program equips trainees with the necessary skills to navigate digital platforms securely while upholding journalistic integrity and ethics.
  • Cooperatives: In a rapidly digitizing economy, cooperatives play a vital role in economic development. Our digital marketing training helps cooperatives enhance their online presence, reach wider audiences, and boost economic sustainability.
  • Content Creators: Morocco's vibrant creative community thrives on digital platforms. Our training focuses on personal branding, ethics in content creation, and leveraging digital tools for social innovation, empowering creators to make meaningful contributions while maintaining professional standards.
  • Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs): Effective digital communication is essential for NGOs to amplify their impact and engage with stakeholders. Our program provides NGOs with the skills and strategies to leverage digital channels for advocacy, fundraising, and community outreach.

Driven by a commitment to the SDGs, particularly Goal 4 (Quality Education), Goal 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth), Goal 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure), and Goal 17 (Partnerships for the Goals), the Digital Clinic aims to foster digital inclusion, economic empowerment, and sustainable development in Morocco.

Through robust research, strategic partnerships, and targeted training initiatives, the Digital Clinic empowers individuals and organizations to harness the power of digital technology for positive change, advancing Morocco's development agenda while contributing to global sustainability efforts.
